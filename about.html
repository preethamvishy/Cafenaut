ARTISANAL CAFE
WITH DEEP ROOTED TRADITIONS.

Cafenaut is an artisanal cafe that values its traditions and its craftspersons.

VISION

We strive to create an immersive refuelling experience for our patrons and visitors. We draw inspiration from several traditions and our culturally diverse baristas and other cafenauts help us deliver authentic experiences. All of our items are prepared in-house with organic ingredients procured from the best sources.


ETHOS

Whether you want to grab a cup of coffee suring your morning run, eat a quick breakfast, lounge around with people you know, have a casual business meeting, work all day, relax in the evening, get your shot of cafeine before you workout or just want to do your thing, we would love to welcome and host you.


LOCATION

Nestled in the heart of Boston, Cafenaut is easily accessible from Green line stations, Orange line stations, multiple bus routes and inter-city rail.
